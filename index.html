Who are we?

Through a free, accessible, and interactive app people of all ages and backgrounds will learn Indigenous languages with the craft of storytelling, art, and song. We aim to foster intergenerational knowledge exchange, and celebrate the linguistic diversity that enriches the first cultures of this country and eventually for Indigenous peoples around the world.
